About Me.

Additionally, I am on the Texas A&M cross country and track and field team- view my profile and the team roster. My time as a student-athlete has taught me the importance of effective communication, how to work with a team to achieve a common goal, and time management skills. This position has helped me better understand the content learned in previous classes and improve my communication skills.


Despite managing a rigorous course load and a busy practice schedule, I also launched and operate a Christian small business called He Before Me- you can read more about my business's website under the projects tab above. Launching my business has sparked my interest in developing websites, and I am seeking to explore this field and gain experience. The mission of FCA is to "see every coach and athlete enter into a growing relationship with Jesus Christ and His church." My role as an FCA leader entails planning and leading small group discussions for our Monday night meeting, facilitating guest speakers, and organizing fellowship nights. My time in the organization has been super impactful and built leadership and communication skills.
